Data dictionary: Hospitality Sector Dataset 2025 (Hotels_2025)

Note: The data associated with this dataset reflects worldwide hospitality sector scenario. Therefore, the findings should be localized to the Sri Lanka Hotel and Tourism Sector.
Variable Name	Data Type	Description
RoomsAvailable		Number of rooms available in the hotel. Hotels range from ~50 rooms (small boutique) to ~300 rooms (large property). Count (rooms)
OccupancyRate		Proportion of rooms occupied during the month. Values are capped between 0.40 (40%) and 1.00 (100%). Proportion (0–1)
ADR	Float	Average Daily Rate (ADR) – average revenue earned per occupied room per day. Higher ADR indicates premium pricing. USD ($)
MarketingSpend	Float	Monthly spending on marketing and promotions (ads, campaigns, partnerships).
StaffCount	Integer	Approximate number of staff working in the hotel, roughly proportional to the number of rooms (~1 staff per 5 rooms). Count (employees)
HotelQualityRank	Categorical (String)	A derived hotel quality ranking based on ADR and OccupancyRate.
• High → ADR > 130 and OccupancyRate > 0.75
• Medium → ADR ≥ 100 and OccupancyRate ≥ 0.60
• Low → ADR < 100 or OccupancyRate < 0.60
GuestSatisfactionScore	Float	Average monthly guest satisfaction rating, simulated based on hotel quality rank (higher for better hotels).
LoyaltyMembers	Integer	Number of registered loyalty program members, correlated with hotel size and quality (higher for bigger, better-ranked hotels).
Revenue	Float	Total monthly revenue, primarily derived from rooms sold (RoomsAvailable × OccupancyRate × ADR × 30 days), plus marketing impact and random variation. USD ($)
